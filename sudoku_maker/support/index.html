Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where are exported files saved?
    Exports are saved to the location you choose in the iOS Files picker.
  • Can I export multiple puzzles per page?
    Yes. You can choose layouts such as 1, 2, 4, or 6 puzzles per page (depending on your settings).
  • What file formats can I export?
    You can export PDF for printing, plus SVG and JSON for flexible reuse.
  • My PDF looks small or large. How can I fix it?
    Try a different layout (puzzles per page) or paper size option if available, then export again.

Troubleshooting

  • Export fails or nothing happens
    Restart the app and try again. Make sure you have enough free storage space.
  • The Files picker closes unexpectedly
    Try exporting to a different folder (e.g., On My iPad) and confirm the folder is writable.
  • Generation is slow
    Reduce the batch size or max attempts/tries (if available), then generate again.
  • I found a bug
    Email us with steps to reproduce and (if possible) a screenshot.
