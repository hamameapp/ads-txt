Privacy Policy

Sudoku PDF Maker

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Summary

Sudoku PDF Maker is a puzzle generation app that lets you create and export files (PDF/SVG/JSON) on your device. We aim to collect as little data as possible.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information.

  • No account required.
  • No name, email address, phone number, or precise location is collected.

Files you export (PDF/SVG/JSON) are created and saved locally on your device or in locations you choose.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app may store app settings and generation history locally (for example: selected grid size, difficulty ranges, and export options). This information stays on your device unless you delete the app or clear its data.

Analytics

By default, Sudoku PDF Maker does not use third-party analytics.

If we enable analytics in a future version, we will update this policy and describe what is collected and how you can opt out (if available).

Advertising

Sudoku PDF Maker does not display third-party ads.

If we add advertising in a future version, we will update this policy and disclose the ad networks used and what data they may process.

Third-Party Services

Sudoku PDF Maker does not share your personal information with third parties.

If you use system features such as iCloud Drive, Files, AirDrop, or printing, those services are provided by Apple and are governed by Apple’s policies.

Children’s Privacy

Sudoku PDF Maker does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the app. Since the app does not collect personal data, privacy risk is minimized.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
